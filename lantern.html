Lantern Festival

Switch to light/dark mode

Lantern Festival is on the 15th day of the first month in a Chinese lunar year. In other words, it is 15 days after the Chinese New Year. It usually falls in February or early March on the Gregorian calendar.

The most common traditional food on the Lantern Festival is Tangyuan (汤圆) or Yuanxiao (元宵), which is a kind of glutinous rice ball. Tangyuan could be either sweet or salty, depending on the fillings in it.

Customs include dragon dance and riddle games (猜灯谜). The riddles are pasted on the lanterns. Players would try to solve the riddles given the themes of the riddles.
